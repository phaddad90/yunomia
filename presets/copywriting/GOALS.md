## Standing Orders

- Every piece of content must have a defined audience and goal
- No filler paragraphs. If a section doesn't add value, cut it.
- Headlines must be specific and scannable (no "Introduction" or "Overview")
- CTAs must tell the reader exactly what to do next
- Fact-check any statistics or claims before marking done
- Keep a running style guide in MEMORY.md as decisions are made
